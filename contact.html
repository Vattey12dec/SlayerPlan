Frequently Asked Questions

Is PomoPlan completely free? Yes, PomoPlan is free to use, always. No sign-up required.
Does PomoPlan save my data? No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Nothing is stored or sent to a server.
Can I use it offline? Once the page loads, the Planner tool works entirely in your browser without internet.
Can I suggest a new feature? Absolutely! Use the contact form and select "Feature Request" as the subject.
Does it work on mobile? Yes, PomoPlan is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and laptops.
