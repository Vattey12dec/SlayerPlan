What is PomoPlan?

Your personalised study session advisor

PomoPlan is a website that helps you manage time and stay focused. It combines task planning with a focus timer so you can stay organised, avoid distractions, and complete your work more efficiently.

By taking inputs such as your energy level, time available, deadline urgency, and sleep hours, PomoPlan generates a study plan for the day — including Pomodoro blocks and suggested break timing for each session.

  1. Open the Planner page
  2. Rate your energy level using the slider (1–10)
  3. Enter last night's sleep hours and time available today
  4. Select your deadline urgency level
  5. Optionally add your subject or task name
  6. Hit "Build My Study Plan" and start studying!
