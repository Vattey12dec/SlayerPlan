Study Tips

Get the most out of every session

Silence all notifications

Put your phone on Do Not Disturb. A single notification costs up to 23 minutes of refocus time.

Hydrate before you start

Dehydration reduces cognitive performance by up to 10%. Always keep water at your study spot.

Move during long breaks

A short walk during a long break improves memory consolidation and recharges your energy.
